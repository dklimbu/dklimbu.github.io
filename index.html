SHARP PACK

Non-adiabatic dynamics simulation software package that features modular design, incorporates nuclear quantum effects via Feynman's imaginary time path integral, employs fewest-switches and ring polymer surface hopping dynamics, and includes built-in model Hamiltonians for method development and assessment.  

EC-MOFs Database

Investigate 2D bulk and mono-layer crystal structures of Metal-Organic Frameworks (MOFs) and explore their tabulated properties through the dedicated EC-MOFs web interface created for 2D MOFs with the ultimate goal of providing a platform for accelerated material design and discovery.
